Target Audience

The target audience of this website are people already passionate about VR and also to get new people indoctrinated into VR

Personas:

VR Veteran

This is the person that has had a headset since 2016 and shows all their friends what it's like every chance they get. They come to this website to keep up with the latest news or just to get my take on things they already know about.

VR Newcomer

This is the person that has never touched a VR headset and doesn't know the difference between a google cardboard, an Oculus Quest, or a Valve Index. They found this site through a google search or were refered to it by a friend. It is our job to get them up to spead an as excited about VR as possible.

Senarios

  1. Daily Updates

    2. This website provides daily updates for those that want them, so recurring visitors always have somthing to look forward to.

  2. Breaking news story

    3. Occasionally something interesting happens in the VR space and they will be reported on here. So someone searching for that will possibly find this site and be imformed.

  3. Reccomeneded Games

    4. It can sometimes be hard to find new VR games and experences, so this website will have a list of only the best for those need to check out.

  4. Reccomeneded Headsets

    5. It can be hard to keep up with all the different VR headsets releasing these days so the site will provide a curated list that goes over what each one has to offer and what will best suit different users.

  5. Reccomeneded Creators

    6. Right now the VR community is on the smaller side so creators have to stick together. If someone is looking for other creators to follow, we will gladly direct them to some of our favorites.
